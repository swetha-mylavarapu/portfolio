User Interview
We conducted few interviews among our newly joined employeesto understand the pain points, behaviors and motivations
Most important findings from the interviews are
They wanted the program to be fun and interactive

User Research
As a part of user research several top tier companies were consulted to generate a curated list of best practices for employee onboarding.
- Icebreaker sessions.
- Visually represent the company cultures and  values
- Configured laptops are given on the first day.
- Fun  and interactive induction program.
Company 1
- Configured Laptop with your email id and other stuff is given to the employee on the first day.
- Interesting and interactive induction program
- There is less paper work and less sessions.
- Opportunity will be discussed before deploying you into the project.
- Supportive if the employee is willing to learn new skills.
Company 2
- Onboarding Session is 45 minutes.
- Six week bootcamp.
- It allows employees to choose their own teams and projects which they are most passionate about.
