Sketches
In order to evaluate the problem space, I had to venture out and see what are the possible applications and solutions present which solve the similar problem and what are the pain-points faced by users in achieving the goals. I evaluated several features that seemed critical from user surveys and identified which ones the application could capitalize on to have an edge over other applications.

I found that primarily users rely on Youtube channels for guided cooking where the chefs prepare dishes and showcases various stages of preparation for a dish. The other options are through blogs, books and some podcasts. Even then, majority of users don't get the experience of guided cooking using the existing solutions available.
CookBook App is a Guided cooking service primarily focussed for newbies to recreate the authentic taste from their favorite chefs. With current advancements in mobile applications and IoT devices at homes especially in kitchens we leverage on these platforms and frameworks for our application. Our application primarily focus on guidance for the users by seamlessly integrating with the environment and providing guidance during the cook time.
1. Allow users to follow along with the cooking procedure and monitor the environment for keeping track of the status
2. Application to seamlessly integrate with Smart Speakers like Google Home, Alexa, for convenience
3. Reduce the burden for employee by providing all necessary details at one go

Challenge 1
Tracking the Cooking Process
Often during cooking keeping track of the progress is a huge task as the users normally do multi tasking while working/doing household chores while cooking. Our application helps to keep track of the ambience using audio sensors in the phone to record few key elements in cooking and track the progress.
Challenge 3
What type of food is famous in the locality?
The application has a curated list of recipes for the authentic dishes, users can browse the recipes with customised filters available in the application. For ex: users primarily use the application after relocating to a new place or a localility, hence the question "What are famous dishes in the locality?" Answering this question is no longer an issue as users are able to browse and share  their dishes conveniently with the community.
Challenge 2
Seamless integration with devices
The application timer seamlessly integrates with other devices to keep track of the cooking process. The application can be seamlessly integrate with smart devices and provide alerts, reminders, that can enhance the cooking process.
